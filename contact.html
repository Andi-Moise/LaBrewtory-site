FAQS

What makes Labrewtory unique?

We are Moldova’s first and only American-owned craft microbrewery, brewing 100% natural, unfiltered, and unpasteurized beer with unique styles from the U.S., Ireland, and the UK.

What types of beer do you brew?

We focus on American, Irish, and UK styles but also create Belgian, German, and Australian-inspired beers, with over 90 unique recipes so far.

Where can I find Labrewtory beer?

Our beers are available in select bars, restaurants, and specialty shops in Moldova—follow us on social media for updates!
