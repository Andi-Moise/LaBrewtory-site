A Decade of Shiner

Small Town, Big Dreams.

Our story is steeped in the rich history of brewing, rooted in the heart of Texas since 1909, but it’s also a tale of constant evolution and anticipation of what lies ahead. From our humble beginnings, we’ve upheld the time-honored traditions of quality brewing that put us on the map. But we’re not content with resting on our laurels. As we journey forward, we’re embracing innovation, exploring new flavors, and continuing to perfect our methods, promising an even brighter future for Shiner Beer.
