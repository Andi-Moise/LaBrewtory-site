A Decade of LaBRewtory

Small Town, Big Dreams.

Founded in late 2017, by American couple Bill and DeEva and their hard working Moldovan team, Labrewtory is Moldova's first and only American owned craft microbrewery. The co-founders arrived in Moldova in 2011, and after some years of working here, decided to stay and start a business. Avid craft beer “home brewers” in America, they decided to put that knowledge to the test and Labrewtory Brewing Company was born.
